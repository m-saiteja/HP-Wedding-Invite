They had a brief conversation — the kind that feels like a prologue to a very long, very beautiful story. A few words. Some laughter. And in that quiet moment between prayers and blessings, something clicked.

He said yes. She said yes. And the stars above quietly rearranged themselves into the shape of a heart.
